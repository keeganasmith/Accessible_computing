Questions to ask your healthcare provider

  • What condition could be causing these symptoms?
  • Which symptoms should be treated as urgent?
  • What daily skin-care steps are safest for me?
  • How often should I monitor this area?

Weekly tracking template

Use this checklist during each skin check:

  • Date and time of check
  • Body area checked
  • Pain level from 0 to 10
  • Changes in color, size, or texture
  • Photo or voice note captured
  • Follow-up action taken
