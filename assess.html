This tool helps you organize symptoms and decide how quickly to contact care. It cannot diagnose conditions, and it does not replace a clinician.

Step 3: Decide next steps

Monitor at home (low concern)

Mild symptoms and no warning signs. Re-check in 24 to 48 hours and keep notes.

Book a visit soon (moderate concern)

Persistent or worsening symptoms. Schedule primary care or dermatology within a few days.

Seek urgent care now (high concern)

Any emergency sign or rapid deterioration. Do not wait for routine appointments.
