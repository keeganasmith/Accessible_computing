Step 3: AI analysis summary (demo)

The model reviews each photo for visible concern signals and generates a triage-oriented output.

Low concern pattern

Features appear mild and stable. Monitor for 24 to 48 hours and recheck if changes occur.

Moderate concern pattern

Persistent inflammation or suspicious visual changes. Book primary care or dermatology soon.

High concern pattern

Possible infection, rapid deterioration, or dangerous skin changes. Seek urgent in-person care now.

Optional context to improve triage quality

Combining image analysis with symptom details helps prioritize follow-up urgency, but diagnosis still requires a clinician.

Step 4: Act on the result

  • Monitor: Retake photos on a regular schedule to track change over time.
  • Book soon: Share results and photos with primary care or dermatology.
  • Urgent: Go to urgent care or emergency services immediately.
