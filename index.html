Understand possible skin issues with confidence

DermAssist Audio offers voice-led instructions to help you inspect your skin, document symptoms, and decide when to contact a clinician. It does not replace a medical diagnosis.

Guided skin check (demo flow)

  1. Choose body area and symptom type using keyboard or voice controls.
  2. Follow short audio prompts to examine color, texture, pain, and warmth.
  3. Record notes, voice memos, and photos with alt text support from a trusted helper.
  4. Get a plain-language summary that highlights urgent warning signs.

Tip: Press Tab to move forward, Shift + Tab to move backward, and Enter to activate selected controls.

Built for real accessibility

  • Voice-first prompts

    Short step-by-step spoken instructions with pause, repeat, and speed controls.

  • High contrast visuals

    Large text, simple layouts, and strong contrast for low-vision support.

  • Screen-reader optimized

    Semantic headings, landmarks, descriptive labels, and clear focus indicators.

  • Caregiver sharing

    Securely share recent notes with a trusted person before appointments.

Urgent warning signs

If any of the following are present, contact emergency or urgent care right away:

  • Rapidly spreading rash, severe swelling, or trouble breathing.
  • Skin infection signs such as fever, pus, or red streaks.
  • A mole or spot that suddenly changes shape, color, or bleeds.
  • Painful burns or wounds that are not healing.
