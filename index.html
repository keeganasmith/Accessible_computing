Point, capture, and understand possible skin concerns

DermAssist Audio guides users through taking clear photos of skin areas and sends those images to an AI model that flags possible issues, explains confidence level, and recommends whether to monitor, book care soon, or seek urgent help.

Start photo assessment Review warning signs

How the photo triage flow works

  1. Use spoken instructions to frame the skin area and capture one or more photos.
  2. Confirm image clarity with vibration/audio feedback and retake if needed.
  3. AI reviews visible patterns (color, border, texture, inflammation, asymmetry).
  4. Receive a plain-language summary with urgency guidance and next-step suggestions.

Tip: Press Tab to move forward, Shift + Tab to move backward, and Enter to activate selected controls.

What the AI check focuses on

  • New or changing spots

    Highlights features that may need dermatologist follow-up, especially evolving lesions.

  • Rash and irritation patterns

    Flags signs of inflammation, infection risk, or contact-related reactions.

  • Wound and healing status

    Identifies visual cues that suggest delayed healing or worsening skin breakdown.

  • Priority guidance

    Sorts results into monitor, schedule soon, or urgent care categories.

Important safety notes

  • AI output is a triage aid, not a diagnosis.
  • If there is severe pain, breathing trouble, fever, or rapidly spreading skin changes, seek urgent care now.
  • Always share concerning photos and symptoms with a licensed healthcare professional.

Accessibility commitments

This interface is designed for keyboard and screen-reader use, with high contrast, large text, semantic structure, and voice-first prompts for hands-free capture.
